## Prompts

1. Restate the core idea of **cocotb scoreboard** in one sentence.
2. In your own words, capture this beat: *A scoreboard checks that what the DUT actually did matches what you expected—transaction by transaction. In cocotb you push golden values onto an expect queue before the response arrives. When a transaction…*
3. Enqueue expect `0xA5`, observe match, then a deliberate mismatch — what should fail?
4. Explain empty-queue observe as a bug class.
